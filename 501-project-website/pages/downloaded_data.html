Downloaded Data from website

In this project, I will use two datasets from the RAND Health Insurance Experiment (HIE). Here you have demographic information about the subjects in the study and also health variables (outcomes) both before and after the experiment. The other file here has information about health care spending. Both dataset consists of 319 variables and 20203 observations. From 1974 to 1982, the RAND Health Insurance Experiment followed 3,958 people aged 14 to 61 from six areas of the country. The participants were randomly assigned to one of 14 insurance plans that had a variety of provisions related to cost-sharing.
